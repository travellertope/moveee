Frame 2: Directory Drawer
DIRECTORY STUDIO

Bisi Ceramics

STUDIO
Added 14 May 2026

Award-winning ceramics studio in Lagos making ritual objects and everyday wares inspired by Yoruba tradition.

Founded by Adaeze Obi in 2019, the studio sources its clay entirely from Ondo state and fires pieces in a traditional kiln built behind the main workshop. They offer monthly public workshops and sell directly from their showroom.

The New Wave of Lagos Street Style

By Fola Olawale · June 9, 2026

A deep dive into the alté scene and its global influence. What started as an underground movement in Lagos has now become the blueprint for global streetwear aesthetics.

We spent three days following the designers, stylists, and creatives who are building brands that bypass traditional European houses to connect directly with a new, diasporic elite.

This isn't just about clothes; it's about reclaiming the narrative of African luxury.
