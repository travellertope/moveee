About the event

Join us for an intimate Amapiano takeover at one of London's most iconic live music venues. We're bringing the authentic sounds of South Africa straight to Camden, featuring a curated lineup of resident DJs and special guest performers.

Expect two rooms of music, bespoke visual installations, and a late licence until 3am. This is a celebration of the genre's global evolution and its deep roots in the diaspora.

"We wanted to create a space where the music isn't just heard, but felt. Jazz Cafe provides that perfect, intimate container for this sound."
— Moveee Lagos Organising Committee
